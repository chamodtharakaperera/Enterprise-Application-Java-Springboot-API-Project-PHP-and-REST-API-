Corporate Information

Our Corporate Vision

"To be the preferred supplier for the Garment Manufacturers and Exporters of Sri Lanka through exceptional operational excellence"


Dayaratne Holdings (Pvt) Ltd has been operating as the largest supplier of Industrial Sewing Machines and ancillary equipment to Apparel Industry for nearly 40 years.

Company has a proven track record of providing technical advice on replacement of existing machinery with multi-functional labour saving machines in order to enhance productivity.

Company maintains customer oriented culture and offers machinery and equipment on monthly rental basis.

Company has a well trained, experienced workforce of 60, including professionals in key positions. The management team is dedicated to provide highest standard of business ethics and corporate governance.

Dayaratne Holdings (Pvt) Ltd is the sole agent for well known "Tenjoy" brand and authorized dealer for Kansai, Pegasus, Juki, Brother, Singer, Eastman and Treasure brands which are imported from Japan, Singapore, Hongkong and China.

Company firmly believes in corporate social responsibilities and has made significant contribution towards community projects.
